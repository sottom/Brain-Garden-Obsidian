# what is best-practice for helping your organization thrive with AI.


1. Someone (business) comes up with an idea.
2. Idea is vetted, determine if it has value.
3. Idea is given to product owner/they were a part of ideation.
4. Works with Solutions Architect to design visual and technical implementation needs.
5. Requirements are documented and estimated. 
6. Timelines are discussed/locked-in.
7. ==Development begins.==
8. ==Dev tests happen.==
9. ==Code is reviewed and approved.==
10. Code is merged to dev.
11. QA of some sort happens.
12. Merged into UAT.
13. UAT happens.
14. It's deployed.
