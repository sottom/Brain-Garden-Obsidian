_Example:_ Joe is at $74k with $300 already accrued towards his next F&D Bucks award. He spends $2k at the store:

- **First $1k (→ $75k, still Gold $50/$1,000):** $700 of that $1k completes the open $1,000 award mark and awards **$50 F&D Bucks**; the remaining $300 carries accrues towards his next F&D bucks, and he hits the $75k Platinum switch. He now is at $75k spend and has $300 of $1,000 accrued towards the next F&D Bucks award.
- **Second $1k (→ $76k, now Platinum $75/$1,000):** Because Joe has not chosen his election, the **$1k is stored as "Pending" accrual**.
- **Platinum upgrade bonus.** When Joe upgrades to the Platinum tier he will also be granted a **$75 tier-upgrade bonus** when he elects to use F&D bucks (separate from the $75/$1,000 accrual award above).

Joe waits **10 days** to change his election.
- If he **chooses F&D Bucks**, the pending $1000 is evaluated and Joe is granted the first $75 F&D Bucks. He is also granted the bonus **$75** F&D Bucks for upgrading to platinum.
- If he **chooses rebate**, the FULL pending $1000 is awarded at the platinum 5% rate. The bonus $75 F&D bucks for crossing tier is dropped.

So spend below the breakpoint earns at the prior tier's rate and spend above it earns at the new tier's rate, with the in-progress $1,000 remainder carried across rather than reset; the tier-upgrade bonus is dropped, while the per-$1,000 award is held pending the F&D-vs-rebate election.
